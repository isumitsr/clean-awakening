    • what is clean awakening ?

    Clean Awakening is a moment of transition that exists in a small silver of time. A cultural resting-stop between the last hundred years past and the next hundred years coming. It is a human moment where a generation becomes internally aware of the past decades of mindless consumption, over-indulgence of the quick, cheap & artificial and glorification of the objectified gaze.

    This is when you decide to
    unlearn, undo, undress
    years of artifice and adopt a clean way of being, doing and seeing.

    You make choices for yourself that apart from taking care of you takes care of the delicate balance in which the beauty and the treasures of the world exists. You give us as you take. You care, nurture, respect and take pride in all that is natural in yourself first and then everywhere else.

    Clean Awakening

    is that consciousness. The feeling that makes you choose things that are good for your soul, mind, body, pride, identity, culture, community, humanity, the earth and its abundance.
